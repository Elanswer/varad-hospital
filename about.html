Who We Are

Committed to Excellence in Healthcare

Varad Hospital is a premier multispeciality hospital located in Chikhali, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Pune. We are dedicated to providing compassionate, high-quality, and affordable medical care to our community.

Our facility is equipped with modern medical technology and staffed by a team of highly experienced specialists and dedicated healthcare professionals. Whether you need a routine checkup or emergency care, Varad Hospital is here for you 24/7.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, patient-centric healthcare with the highest standards of ethics and clinical excellence.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare partner in the region, recognized for advanced care and compassionate healing.

Medical Leadership

A Commitment to Compassionate Care

"At Varad Hospital, we believe that true healthcare goes beyond treating symptoms—it’s about treating the person. Our goal has always been to bring world-class medical facilities and expertise closer to home for the people of Chikhali and Pimpri-Chinchwad.
