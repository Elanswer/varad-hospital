Laboratory Specialties

Hematology

Study of blood, blood-forming organs, and blood diseases.

Histopathology

Microscopic examination of tissue in order to study the manifestations of disease.

Biochemistry

Chemical processes within and relating to living organisms.

Microbiology

Study of microscopic organisms, such as bacteria, viruses, archaea, fungi and protozoa.

Immunology

Study of the immune system in all organisms.

Molecular Biology

Molecular basis of biological activity in and between cells.

Serology

Scientific study of serum and other body fluids.

Clinical Pathology

Diagnosis of disease based on the laboratory analysis of bodily fluids.
